EncryptionToolbox

visit https://github.com/pg0/EncryptionToolbox

The following options are available

Synchronous Encryption

  • AES-EAX (openpgp.js)
  • AES256 (CryptoJS)

Asynchronous Encryption

  • Generate KeyPair (ECC/RSA)
  • PGP Encrypt
  • PGP Encrypt for multiple Keys
  • PGP Decrypt
  • PGP Sign and Verify Text
